Salou open 2022le 23 mai

It had not been held since 2010, Covid obliges. From 22 to 30 May, near the sunny beaches of Spain, 88 players from 14 countries, including 19 French and several from our League, played this tournament in 9 rounds at normal pace.

Tournoir Mondiale

The last tournament was held on May 18, 2019 due to the pandemic, so a big congratulations to all participants (5 clubs represented: Bourg, St Etienne, Bourgoin, Léman and Lyon) to this 29th edition of philanthropic tournament of HCL Congratulations to the winners and to the winners of the tombola (Claude, connected watch, Jean-Claude, FM radio, books for Roger, Jean-Marie etc.) 70 participants (54 chess players and 16 checkers players) in all to this beautiful edition of recovery. And a big thank you to President Richard for all the work. See the League table Translated with www.DeepL.com/Translator (free version)

Nation games du Quebec

It takes place in Antalya, Turkey from May 1 to 9 with 10 teams of three players from 4 continents. France is represented by Arnaud Cordier, Kevin Machtelink and Fidèle Nimbi. In the standard games, in the last round France lost the first place against Holland, which won the tournament in front of France and Senegal. The fast games take place on May 7 and the blitz on May 8.
